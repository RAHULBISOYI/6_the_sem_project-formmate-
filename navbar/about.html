About 📋 FormMate

FormMate is a citizen-focused web application designed to simplify and automate the form-filling process. The platform assists users by analyzing uploaded forms and guiding them through accurate and complete form submission.

With FormMate, users can upload any government or institutional form to the website. The system intelligently identifies the required fields and supporting documents, and then automatically fills the form using the information and documents provided by the user.

Key features include:

  • Upload and intelligent analysis of forms.
  • Automatic form filling to reduce manual effort.
  • Smart recommendations for required supporting documents.
  • Error reduction and improved submission accuracy.
  • Simple and accessible interface for all citizens.

FormMate aims to save time, reduce mistakes, and improve accessibility by automating form completion and ensuring that users upload the correct documents, leading to faster and more successful form submissions.
